The Story Of Our Company


We are Daniel, Marie and Ignacio. We all three came from different experiences and backgrounds but we share one common goal, try to reduce pollution. We decided one of the best way to fix this was to create an eco friendly battery. Consequently in March 2020 Hotza Technology was born. We leased a building in Dublin, got some scary bank loans, spent all our money on ideas and proyects. Our biggest mission when we set up Hotza Technology was to make other people as passionate about environment as we are. And that is still our biggest mission today.

DEVICE

Innovation

Rain as Energy

If you can make energy from wind, why not from rainwater? In the water cycle, water evaporates via solar energy and gains potential energy that is then lost again when the water precipitates.Our smart device generates electric power from raindrops.

Solar Charger

Our solar charge are placed around the city and allows people to recharged their batteries during the day.

Charge while in Motion

Wind power or wind energy is the use of wind to provide mechanical power through wind turbines to turn electric generators for electrical power. We have now introduced our self charging battery while driving. Wind power is a popular sustainable, renewable source of power that has a much smaller impact on the environment compared to burning fossil fuels.

3IN1

SMALL BUT POWERFULL

Our 3IN1 intelligent batteries have a multi-stage cycle that analyses, charges, protects and repairs batteries. This means that instead of just adding charge, the battery charger responds to the condition of the battery, then applies the fastest charge available using either the rain, the wind or the sun as energy.
